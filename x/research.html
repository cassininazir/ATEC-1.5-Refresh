ArtSciLab

The UT Dallas ATEC & EMAC ArtSciLab carries out initiatives that require collaboration between artists and scientists, applied to problems of cultural timeliness and societal urgency.

Leonardo Initiatives
STEM to STEAM

As part of the international movement to integrate the arts, humanities and design into STEM education and research strategies, we develop innovative methodologies for translating current research into teaching and public contexts. Projects have included the ATEC STEAM Summer Camp and the UTD STEAM Working Group.
